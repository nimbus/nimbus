## You may

- use the word `Nimbus` in truthful, nominative ways to refer to the project
- say that your software is `compatible with Nimbus`
- link to the Nimbus repository, docs, and releases

## You may not

- name your fork, service, or company in a way that suggests it is official
  Nimbus
- use Nimbus logos or branding without permission
- market a hosted or managed service as if it were the official Nimbus service
- imply sponsorship, endorsement, partnership, or certification without written
  permission

## Forks

If you distribute or publicly host a fork, you must give it a distinct name and
branding. You may describe it as a fork of or compatible with Nimbus, but not
as Nimbus itself.

## Questions

For trademark questions or permission requests, contact the current repository
owner or Nimbus maintainers through the project's published contact channel.
