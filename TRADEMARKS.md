- name your fork, service, or company in a way that suggests it is official

## Forks

If you distribute or publicly host a fork, you must give it a distinct name and
branding. You may describe it as a fork of or compatible with Neovex, but not
as Neovex itself.

owner or Neovex maintainers through the project's published contact channel.
